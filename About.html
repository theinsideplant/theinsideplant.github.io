The Inside Plant was started in July 2025, as the owner’s first ever site. The Inside Plant is fully focused on providing everybody with the proper knowledge of indoor plants and how to care for them. We want your house to be a home, by brightening it up with a little piece of nature. Having plants in your home offers many benefits, including reduced stress, improved air quality, and enhanced mood. Plants help to clean the air around us by absorbing carbon dioxide, they can increase humidity and filter dust, and they release fresh oxygen. Studies have shown that indoor plants can decrease depressive moods, boost focus, and lessen symptoms of anxiety, contributing to you/your household’s well-being.
